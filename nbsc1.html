Vision
The NBSC Foundation can take action on a wide variety of issues due to its unique character and the powers vested in its charter, which is considered a National Organization. As such, the NBSC Charater is an instrument of National law and NBSC Member Stated are bound by it The NBSC Charter codifies the major principles of National & State relations.
Since the NBSCs founding in 2021, the mission and work of the Organization have been guided by the purposes and principles contained in its , which has been amended one time in 2021.
